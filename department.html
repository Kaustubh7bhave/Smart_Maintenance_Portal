Smart Maintenance Portal

  1. View Complaints: The department can view all complaints that have been submitted by users.
  2. Close Complaints: The department can close a complaint with a reason or comment, indicating that the issue has been resolved.
  3. Update Status: The department can update the status of a complaint, indicating whether it is still in progress or has been resolved.
  4. Notifications: The department will receive notifications as per the schedule added by the admin, so they can stay informed about new complaints or updates to existing ones.
